Prices and Percentages

There is one thing in life that everyone despises—taxes. But there is something that takes the sting away: saving money.

From recycling aluminum cans to using coupons, saving money is possible. However, sometimes people don't have the time (insert life's woes). Thankfully, this application will save time, which will save you "moola," "bread," "cheddar," "lettuce"—money!

In the first field, insert the current price of a product you're eyeballing. In the second field, increase or decrease its current price, showing you how much "bacon" you will save (or lose).

Enjoy! Spend responsibly.
